Awareness & Prevention
How to spot phishing
Check the sender's email address carefully. Look for spelling errors, unexpected attachments, and urgent requests for personal info. Hover over links to preview the actual destination.
What to do if hacked
Immediately change passwords, enable 2FA, contact your bank to freeze accounts if necessary, and report the incident on cybercrime.gov.in.
Identifying UPI Fraud
Remember: You only need to enter your UPI PIN to SEND money, never to RECEIVE money. If someone asks you to scan a QR code to receive a payment, it is a scam.
